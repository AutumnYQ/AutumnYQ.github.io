Yuqiu Liu (刘育秋)

My name is Yuqiu Liu (刘育秋). I am a PhD student in Computing Science at Simon Fraser University, advised by Prof. Wuyang Chen. My current research interests lie in 3D/4D fluid reconstruction.

I got my MS degree in Beijing Forestry University. I was working in Tsinghua Laboratory of Brain and Intelligence for one year, Qiyuan Lab for two years, advised by Prof. Xiaolin Hu and Prof. Liang Tang.
